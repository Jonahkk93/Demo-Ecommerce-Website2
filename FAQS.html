Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

General Questions

1. What products do you offer?

We offer a wide range of hair and nail products, including shampoos, conditioners, hair treatments, styling tools, nail polishes, nail care kits, and accessories.

2. Are your products suitable for all hair and nail types?


Yes! We carry products for different hair textures, types, and concerns, as well as nail care products for natural and artificial nails.

3. Do you offer vegan or cruelty-free products?

Yes, we have a selection of vegan and cruelty-free hair and nail products. You can find them in the Vegan & Cruelty-Free section of our website. Orders & Shipping

4. How do I place an order?

Simply browse our website, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You will receive an email confirmation once your order is placed.

5. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit/debit cards, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and other secure payment options.

6. Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship to many countries. Shipping costs and delivery times vary based on location. You can check shipping options at checkout.

7. How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping typically takes 5–7 business days, while expedited shipping options are available for faster delivery.

Returns & Refunds

8. What is your return policy? We accept returns within 30 days of purchase if the product is unused and in its original packaging. Some exclusions apply.

9. How do I start a return?

Contact our customer service team with your order number, and we will guide you through the return process.

10. When will I receive my refund?

Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days after we receive and inspect the returned product.
